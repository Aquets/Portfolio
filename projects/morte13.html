Death. 13 solid doubts

Web design - 2020

Website for the exibition "Death. 13 solid doubts, death, speculation and communicative machine" in which were shown the projects made by the students of final synthesis course at Politecnico di Milano bachelor degree.

The projects are 13 speculations translated into communicative machines. Artifacts, devices, interactions, stories as a result of an experience of learning, knowledge and growth.
