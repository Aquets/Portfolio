Click, spegnete la luce

Video / illustration - 2020

Click is an animated short film about the adventure of a little kid, passionate about Space, and his desire to see the stars in the night sky.

The project is meant to communicate to kids from 6 to 10 years old about reasons and effects of light pollution and, most importantly, about the importance of reciprocal and unconditional help to make the world a better place. Through the process, it was decided to focus the narrative on the simple act of turning the light off, turning it into an heroic action, showing kids that even the easiest things can be helpful if everyone does it.
