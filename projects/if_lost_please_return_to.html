If lost please return to

Editorial design - 2021

The relationship we have with our wallet is anything but pragmatic. We use it to store everything that has value, and not just in economic terms. Its content, therefore, can reveal a lot of things about who owns it.

What does your wallet tell about you?

We asked ten friends this question, we listened to their answers and we invited them to show us their actual content.

The result is “If lost please return to”, an editorial project that tells the story and identity of ten people through the contents of their wallets.
