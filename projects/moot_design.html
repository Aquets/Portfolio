Moot design

Web design - 2020

Dieter Rams, chief design officer at Braun from 1961 to 1995, wrote the “Ten principles of Good Design”. “Moot Design” is a web zine that wants to counter humorously the paradigm of design as strictly functional and rational.

To do so, it shows the contemporary poor design from an unprecedented point of view, portraying it as worthy and human. The good aspects of the mass production are exalted even in their unexpected implications, and the common products are presented as luxury objects.
