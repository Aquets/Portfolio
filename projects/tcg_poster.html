T.C.G. Trading Card Gambling

Poster design - 2022

Twenty generative posters about the Pokemon trading card game and booster packs, visualizing the chances of finding rare cards. The goal is creating awareness on the strong parallelism between trading card games and gambling.

The posters are generated through a creative coding script using the OPENRNDR framework.

Role: Creative coding and data extraction.
