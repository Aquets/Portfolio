Mirami

Interaction design - 2020

Mirami is a speculative design project that deals with the issue of aging perceived as the prelude to death. the project is divided into two main parts, a magazine that advertises about the best cosmetic products and an installation built for the release of the magazine.

An interactive mirror is the key element of the installation. Thanks to the artificial intelligence it estimates the age of whoever stands in front of it, and brings him to the magazine’s page containing the suitable cosmetic product.

Role: Build the interactive mirror and design the physical installation.
