Saiver

Brand identity / Web design - 2020

Saiver is a worldwide-known conditioning systems company. The project consists in the complete renewal of their visual identity and in the realization of a new website, from concept to coding.

The identity system reflects the double nature of the company, on one side extremely technical while on the other side dynamic and light. To enforce the brand communication, a custom typeface was also created. The characters are solid yet fresh, thanks to the use of strong cuts.
